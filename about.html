Our Story

The Problem

We were frustrated with traditional PR agencies. They charged massive monthly retainers ($10k+) with zero guarantees. They billed for "effort" (pitching emails), not for "outcomes" (published articles).

The Solution

Credence was born to fix this. We treat PR like engineering. We built direct relationships with 500+ publishers to bypass the pitching process entirely. If we don't publish, you don't pay. It's that simple.
