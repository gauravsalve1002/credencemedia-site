Legal Center

Transparency is our core value. Review our operational policies below.

1. Performance Guarantee & Refunds

We operate on a strict pay-for-performance model.

  • Guaranteed Deliverables: If Credence Media Group fails to secure the number of placements outlined in your plan within 30 days of you approving the draft, you are entitled to a 100% refund.
  • Conditions: The guarantee is void if failure to publish is due to Client delays (e.g., taking >48 hours to approve drafts, changing topics mid-process).

2. Editorial Rights & Content Modification

While we draft content to your specifications, the Publication has the final say:

  • Publisher Edits: News outlets reserve the right to edit headlines, body text, and images to align with their specific editorial guidelines and formatting styles.
  • Headline Changes: A publisher may change a headline to make it more "newsworthy" or SEO-friendly. These changes are standard industry practice and are not grounds for a refund as long as the core message remains intact.

3. Article Structure & Tier-1 Guidelines

Different tiers of publications require different content formats:

  • Standard Placements: Typically structured as "Brand Features" or "Sponsored Articles" which allow for more promotional language.
  • Tier-1 Publications (Forbes, Bloomberg, etc.): These outlets have strict editorial guidelines. Content aimed for these platforms must often be structured as a Press Release (newsworthy announcement) or a non-promotional Guest Op-Ed. Purely promotional "sales copy" will be rejected by Tier-1 editors.

4. "Sponsored" Tags & Disclosure

To ensure guaranteed placement and compliance with global advertising laws (FTC/FCC):

  • Disclosure Labels: Some publications may label articles as "Sponsored," "Brand Partner," or "Paid Content." This is a legal requirement for guaranteed placement and ensures your article remains permanent and is not removed for deceptive practices.
  • Organic vs. Paid: While our goal is to make content look as native as possible, the presence of a disclosure tag is a safety measure for your brand's reputation and is not grounds for a refund.

5. Post-Publication Liability (Removal Policy)

  • Service Fulfillment: Our service is considered "fulfilled" once the article is live and the URL is delivered to the Client.
  • Future Removal: While rare, a publisher may remove an article in the future due to site restructuring, policy changes, or if the Client gets involved in a legal scandal. Credence Media Group is not liable for post-publication removal and will not issue refunds for articles that were successfully published and live for at least 48 hours.

6. Cancellation Policy

  • Monthly Plans: Cancel anytime via email 3 business days before your renewal date.
  • Yearly Plans: Yearly plans are committed contracts. Early cancellation does not result in a refund of the remaining balance.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Name, Email, Phone Number.
  • Business Data: Company URL, Goals, and niche information.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Delivering media placements (sending bio to journalists).
  • Structuring data for Knowledge Panels (Google).
  • We never sell your data to third parties.

3. Media Exposure

By using our service, you consent to your provided Bio, Name, and Headshot being made publicly available on news websites and Google databases.
