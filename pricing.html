Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Credence Media different from a PR firm?

Traditional PR focuses on fleeting attention. We focus on permanent digital assets, search authority, and teaching AI models to trust your brand. We combine PR with high-level technical SEO and AI optimization.

Is media coverage guaranteed?

Yes. Our contracts include performance clauses. We control the editorial inventory, ensuring your narrative is published exactly as approved by you.

How fast will I see results?

Editorial features typically go live within 14-21 days. SEO and Knowledge Panel changes can take 30-60 days to fully propagate across Google's index.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes. Our primary markets are India, US, UK, and the Middle East. We tailor the media rotation based on your target geography.
