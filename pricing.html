Pricing Questions

Everything you need to know before investing.

What happens if you fail to place my article? +

You get 100% of your money back. We operate on a strict pay-for-performance model. If we do not secure the guaranteed number of placements promised in your plan within 30 days, we issue a full refund immediately. No questions asked.

Are Forbes or Bloomberg included in the standard plans? +

Our standard plans cover Premium Industry Outlets (e.g., Yahoo Finance, MarketWatch, Digital Journal) which drive the fastest SEO results.

Tier-1 Publications (Forbes, TechCrunch, Bloomberg) are available as "Elite Add-Ons." These networks have strict editorial standards and higher syndication fees. We can pitch you to them, but we first need to audit your brand's current authority to ensure acceptance. Contact us for a Tier-1 audit.

Do I need to write the articles myself? +

No. All plans include professional drafting by our in-house team of former journalists. We interview you to capture your voice/angle, draft the content, and send it to you for unlimited revisions. We only pitch once you approve the final draft.

Can I choose exactly which publication I want? +

We can recommend the best publications based on your industry (Tech, Finance, Lifestyle) to ensure the highest SEO impact. While you can request specific outlets, we guarantee tier-level placement (e.g., "Premium Business Outlet" vs. "Global News"). We will never publish you on a site irrelevant to your brand.

Is there a long-term contract? +

For Monthly plans, there is no long-term contract; you can cancel anytime before your next billing cycle. For Yearly plans (where you save 20%), you are committing to 12 months of authority building in exchange for the discounted rate.

What is AEO & GEO optimization? +

Standard SEO ranks you on Google. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) ensures Siri/Alexa can answer questions about you. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) structures your data so AI models like ChatGPT and Gemini cite your brand as a trusted source. We include both in every plan.
